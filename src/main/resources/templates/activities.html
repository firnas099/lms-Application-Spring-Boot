Student clubs


Rotaract Club

The Rotaract Club of NSBM is one of the 5 Rotaract clubs sponsored by the Rotary Club of Colombo West. The Club was Chartered and Sponsored by us in 2019 and has a membership of 84.

The club was chartered on the 1st of October 2019 with Adheesha Dharmakeerthi leading the club as its first president. The club is one of the most dynamic in the district with a current membership of over 120 which is commendable considering the club is just one year old.
